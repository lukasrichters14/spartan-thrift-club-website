Past Events

Ann Arbor

11/14/20

In Ann Arbor we went to two thrift shops. The first, Ragstock was a more upscale store with a large variety of holiday sweaters. The second, Ann Arbor PTO, was more of a warehouse, but with a fantastic selection of home goods, clothes, and furniture. Best find: storage cubes (pictured here)

Saginaw

10/17/20

Our first outing as Spartan Thrift club! We went to Underground Railroad Resale, and a Salvation Army. The resale shop was a fantastic place, full of a wide assortment of clothes and other trinkets, with an amazing staff. Best find: 2 "pirate grandpa" statues and a bathrobe.
